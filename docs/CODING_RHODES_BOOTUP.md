                # THE KAYFABE PHILOSOPHY

                ## The Booker Model

                Paul teaches that you are not your Babyface (light side) or your Heel (shadow side). You are the **Booker** — the neutral one who listens to both cut promos and decides what gets airtime.

                - Inner turmoil comes from identifying as the Face and rejecting the Heel
                - - The Heel gives drive. The Babyface gives contentment. Both serve the story.
                  - - **Separate feeling from thought.** A gut feeling could be anxiety or excitement — the Booker decides the meaning.
                    - - A Heel getting buried every night isn't good storytelling.
                     
                      - ## The Gimmick Practice
                     
                      - - Give your Heel and Babyface identifiable characters — names, patterns, signature moves
                        - - Recognition is the first step to booking power
                          - - "Oh, that's just [Heel name] cutting his usual promo. I know this one."
                            - - As you uncover more of yourself, the gimmicks grow more complex
                             
                              - ## The Big One
                             
                              - - The Big One is the Booker's North Star
                                - - It's how you evaluate promos: "Does this serve the story I'm telling?"
                                  - - The Heel and Babyface are a tag team who forgot they're on the same side — The Big One reminds them
                                    - - You can't set The Big One until you know who's setting it (Step 1 must come first)
                                     
                                      - ## The Progression
                                     
                                      - 1. **Know Your Character** — Sort what's yours from what's inherited. Gimmick the Heel and Babyface. Ask why at every step.
                                        2. 2. **Live Your Character** — Test the gimmicks. See what pops, what generates heat. Set Main Event goals. Move with intention. Reframe experiences to serve the plot.
                                           3. 3. **Earn The Big One** — Main Event achievement proves you're ready to see the real peak.
                                              4. 4. **Complete the Story** — The Big One isn't a goal achieved. It's a storyline completed. What comes after is yours.
                                                
                                                 5. ## Core Language
                                                
                                                 6. - "Booking a storyline" not "achieving a goal"
                                                    - - "That story is complete"
                                                      - - "What pops" — what resonates
                                                        - - "What generates heat" — friction, but also energy
                                                          - - Life is a pro wrestling federation. As you level up, you earn booking power.
